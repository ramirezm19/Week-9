¡Hola! We are Omar and Mayra. We are a married couple who love to travel.

As Mexican immigrants ourselves, we arrived separately to the States 27 years ago from our homestate of Chihuahua, went to the same high school -- NO, we are not high school sweethearts -- and later, started dating and since then, we've traveled together. More than 12 years ago, here we still are.

Together, we have explored places like Costa Rica, Mesa Verde, Japan, Sayulita, Vietnam, Thailand, Los Cabos, Oaxaca, Vancouver, Mexico City, and many more.

After 2 years of COVID restrictions worldwide, in 2022 we ventured back out, but this time, in a different way. In March 2022, we became the tour/experience guides for a group of 8 American foodie friends. We took inspiration from Netflix' The Taco Chronicles and created an itinerary based on different kinds of tacos, where to eat them, and places to explore around Mexico City.

Then, because we need "Us" time, we travelled to the other side of the world to experience the culture, food, and everything else that Istanbul, Turkey, has to offer.

Enjoy the journey of experiencing CDMX and Istanbul with us!
