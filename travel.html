Travel with Us

In completing our first CDMX trip as tour guides, we've decided to continue on with the tradition. Twice per year, in the Spring and Fall equinoxes, we will take a group of 10 individuals and guide them through this wonderful city.

CDMX in the spring starts with the blooming Jacaranda trees.

CDMX in the Fall can include Independence Day celebrations and Dia de Muertos.

If you are interested in traveling with MXWanderlust to CDMX, enter your email and check the trip you are interested in. We will contact you with trip information, availability, and other details.

We'll never share your email with anyone else.
